Welcome

Welcome to our innovative Digital Twin platform focused on preventive health. Using Smart BMI Health Checkup Standby technology, we provide personalized health insights to help you stay ahead of potential risks.

How It Works

Our system collects real-time BMI and other health data via smart sensors. This data feeds into a digital twin model simulating your health profile, enabling early detection of health risks and personalized recommendations.
